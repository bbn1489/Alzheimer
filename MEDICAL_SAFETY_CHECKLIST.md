# ✅ MEDICAL SAFETY CHECKLIST

## Before Using This Tool

- [ ] I understand this tool analyzes brain imaging data ONLY
- [ ] I understand it CANNOT diagnose from normal photos or selfies
- [ ] I have read MEDICAL_SAFETY_GUIDELINES.md
- [ ] I understand this is research-only, not clinical diagnosis
- [ ] I will not rely solely on this tool for medical decisions
- [ ] I will consult qualified healthcare professionals
- [ ] I understand the proper medical workflow requires:
  - [ ] Patient evaluation by physician
  - [ ] Professional brain imaging (MRI/CT/PET)
  - [ ] Radiologist interpretation
  - [ ] Neurologist assessment
  - [ ] AI model as supplementary evidence
- [ ] I accept full responsibility for proper medical protocols
- [ ] I understand medical professionals remain liable for diagnoses
- [ ] I will not bypass established medical procedures

## Input Validation Checklist

Before running predictions, confirm:

- [ ] Image is from a medical brain imaging scan
- [ ] Image is MRI, CT, or PET scan of the brain
- [ ] Scan obtained from licensed medical facility
- [ ] Scan meets professional imaging standards
- [ ] Scan is not a normal photograph or photo
- [ ] Scan is not a face image or selfie
- [ ] Scan is grayscale or near-grayscale
- [ ] Scan appears to show brain tissue
- [ ] Image file is valid and readable
- [ ] File path is correct

## Usage Checklist

When using the tool:

- [ ] Medical supervisor/ethics board has approved use
- [ ] Institutional review board (IRB) has reviewed protocol
- [ ] Patient consent obtained (if applicable)
- [ ] Image validation has passed (tool will verify)
- [ ] Result will be reviewed by qualified professional
- [ ] Radiologist will interpret the scan
- [ ] Neurologist will evaluate patient
- [ ] Result will NOT be sole basis for diagnosis
- [ ] Proper disclaimers will be displayed
- [ ] Patient will be informed appropriately
- [ ] All medical protocols will be followed
- [ ] Documentation maintained for compliance

## Warning Signs - DO NOT PROCEED If:

- ❌ Using on non-medical images (photos, selfies)
- ❌ Planning to diagnose without professional review
- ❌ No radiologist available for image interpretation
- ❌ No neurologist available for patient evaluation
- ❌ No IRB/ethics board approval for research
- ❌ No institutional legal review completed
- ❌ Patient privacy not protected
- ❌ No informed consent obtained
- ❌ No medical supervision available
- ❌ Using for purposes not approved
- ❌ Bypassing established medical protocols
- ❌ Making claims beyond research/support role
- ❌ Inadequate staff training
- ❌ No proper system validation

## Red Flags - Reassess If:

- ⚠️ High error rate on validation
- ⚠️ Inconsistent results on known scans
- ⚠️ Poor performance on specific demographics
- ⚠️ Staff not following protocols properly
- ⚠️ Patient safety concerns raised
- ⚠️ Unexpected result patterns
- ⚠️ System reliability issues
- ⚠️ Data security concerns
- ⚠️ Compliance violations noted
- ⚠️ User feedback negative
- ⚠️ Professional concerns raised
- ⚠️ Performance metric changes
